# Santhosh Yarns Official Website - Project Idea

## Overview

The **Santhosh Yarns Official Website** will serve as an online platform for customers and admins. Customers can explore yarn products, place orders, and learn about the company, while admins manage inventory and customer orders.

## Objectives

- **For Customers**:
  - Browse yarn threads and services.
  - Place orders after logging in.
  - Contact support for inquiries.

- **For Admins**:
  - Manage yarn inventory (add, edit, delete).
  - Track customer orders and update their statuses.
  
## Features

1. **User Authentication**:
   - Separate login for customers and admins.
  
2. **Product Catalog**:
   - Display yarn threads with details.
   - Admins can manage the catalog.

3. **Order Management**:
   - Customers can place orders.
   - Admins receive and process orders.

4. **Responsive Design**:
   - Mobile-friendly and accessible across devices.

5. **Contact Us**:
   - A page for customer inquiries.

## Tech Stack

- **Frontend**: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React.js
- **Backend**: Node.js, MongoDB


## Conclusion

The **Santhosh Yarns Website** will be an easy-to-use platform that simplifies yarn ordering and inventory management, improving user experience for both customers and admins.
